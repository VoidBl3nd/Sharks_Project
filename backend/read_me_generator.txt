
Tracking Generator
------------------

Generates somewhat random maritime paths coordinates all around the globe.
Each path has a timeline such that it emulates real position of the tracked object at some point in time.

Parameters can be tweaked to get the desired dataset.
Currently, data for 70 dates in 2024 have been generated.



Business Generator
------------------

Generates somewhat random data about sharks attacks carried out by the 'Sharky Cruise' business.

Parameters can be tweaked to get the desired dataset.
Currently, 8 sharks and 4 cruises have been tracked over 100 periods of time.
